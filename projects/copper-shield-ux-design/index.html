Copper Shield

While studying UX design for the first time, I was tasked with developing a way to grow Indigenous business. What I designed was the idea of a digital shield that could help would-be entrepreneurs be and feel safe when getting their business started. This project idea has a lot of room to grow. Because survivors of intimate partner violence shouldn't need to stay small.
