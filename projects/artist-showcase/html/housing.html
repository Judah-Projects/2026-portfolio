Housing as Art

When our homes are designed properly, they become works of art that inhabit the knowledge of yesteryears. These pieces can give an Ancestral foundation to stand on while creating today and planning for tomorrow.

That's what the Skeetchestn (First Nation) Natural Resource Corporation has done for their community, and yours. They have designed a modular building inspired by a combination of powwow-arbour and pit-house.

Inside Show Home:

These works of art can be flat-packed, shipped across Canada, and assembled on a variety of different foundations in endless configurations.

Shipping:

They come in 3 different sizes which make them perfect for generational housing (units can be added each generation), offices, and commercial enterprises like resorts or assisted living.
